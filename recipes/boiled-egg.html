Boiled Egg

Description

Boiled egg is the best food in the planet, as it contains every nutrient you could ever need.

There's no such thing as a bad egg, just a not that good egg.

Ingredients

  • EGG
  • 500ml of water

Steps

  1. Boil the water
  2. Throw the egg in the water
  3. Leave it there for five minutes
  4. Eat the egg
