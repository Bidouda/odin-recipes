Chamomile Tea

Description

Chamomile tea is a herbal tea that tastes very good and it's easy to prepare.

Chamomile tea is not really tea but it might as well be.

Ingredients

  • Two tea bags of chamomile tea
  • 400ml of water
  • 1.5 Teaspoons of sugar

Steps

  1. Boil the water and place it in a mug
  2. Add the two tea bags to it
  3. Let it sit for 5 minutes
  4. Throw in the sugar
