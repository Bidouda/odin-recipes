Chocolate Bar Hot Chocolate

Description

The Chocolate Bar Hot Chocolate recipe offers a delightful way to transform leftover milk chocolate bars into a rich and comforting beverage. By melting a chopped 1.55-ounce milk chocolate bar with ⅔ cup of milk over medium-low heat, and whisking until smooth, you create a luscious base. A pinch of ground cinnamon is then added to enhance the flavor profile, introducing a subtle warmth that complements the sweetness of the chocolate. The result is a velvety drink that captures the essence of indulgence in every sip.

This homemade hot chocolate is not only quick to prepare, taking approximately 10 minutes from start to finish, but also customizable to suit personal preferences. For a creamier consistency, additional milk can be incorporated. Serving it in a cozy mug makes it perfect for chilly days or as a soothing treat anytime. The combination of melted milk chocolate and a hint of cinnamon creates a harmonious blend that elevates the traditional hot chocolate experience, making it a must-try for chocolate enthusiasts.

Ingredients

  • 1 (1.55 ounce) bar milk chocolate, chopped
  • ⅔ cup milk, or more to taste
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place chocolate pieces in a saucepan over medium-low heat
  3. Add milk and whisk constantly until chocolate is melted and well blended, about 5 minutes
  4. Whisk in cinnamon
  5. Remove from heat
  6. Add more milk if desired
  7. Serve in a mug
